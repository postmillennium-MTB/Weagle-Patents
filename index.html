About Dave Weagle

Mechanical engineer (Wentworth Institute of Technology) and co-founder of Evil Bikes and e*thirteen. Applied four-wheeled vehicle dynamics to chain-driven bicycles, producing the first published mathematical characterization of anti-squat and braking-squat. Key platforms: DW-Link (DW4 & DW6), Split Pivot, DELTA, Orion Dynamics, and the Trust/Trvstper linkage front fork.
