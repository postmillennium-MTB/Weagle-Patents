Dave Weagle — Patent Portfolio

MECHANICAL ENGINEER · SUSPENSION INNOVATOR · MARTHA'S VINEYARD, MA

Mechanical engineer (Wentworth Institute of Technology) and co-founder of Evil Bikes and e*thirteen. Applied four-wheeled vehicle dynamics to chain-driven bicycles, producing the first published mathematical characterization of anti-squat and braking-squat. Key platforms: DW-Link (DW4 & DW6), Split Pivot, DELTA, Orion Dynamics, and the Trust linkage front fork.

The core DW-Link patent (US7128329B2) — the foundational anti-squat positioning patent licensed to Iron Horse, Ibis, Pivot, and Turner — expired September 28, 2023. Its claimed suspension geometry is now public domain in the US: any manufacturer may legally produce designs covered by that patent's claims without a license. Note this applies to that specific patent's claims only — later DW-Link-related filings and the separate DW6 work may still be in force.
